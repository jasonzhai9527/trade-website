About Winkstar

Winkstar electronic and lighting co,. LTD is a branch factory set up in Cambodia by Golden Vessel Electronic and Lighting, Inc Located at the residential area, which along Km 69, National Road#2, Daun keo City, Roka Knong District, Prey Prom Village, Takeo province of Cambodia. The total land area occupied by the facility land size is 22,131 square meters and the total building size is 12,261 square meters. It also plans to set up 10 production lines in the new plant. The new factory was registered in 2023. formal production is expected to begin before the end of December. Application will be submitted to UL at the end of 2023 and UL is expected to complete the test in late November or early December. We also plan to complete BSCI, ISO9001, SCAN and other factory certifications at the beginning of next year. The main products of our new factory are: LED decorative light string, Incandescent light string, LED APP light in blue tooth controller (Single color, dual-color, RGB, etc.), LED rope light, Mini Incandescent Rope light, LED soft light strip, LED neon tube light, LED C7 C9 bulb, etc. We will make every effort to build a new factory with “well-equipped facilities and excellent quality control” as our target, Golden Vessel (Cambodia) Electronic & Lighting, Inc should be one of your best choices!
